1.Contact our Service Providers:

If your car is damaged due to an accident or any other covered event, you should get in touch with our Website or Mobile Application first. We will guide you through the claim process and provide information on authorized repair shops.

2.Choose nearby repair shops:

Based on the information provided by you, we suggest you a reputable vehicle repair shop.

3.Schedule an Appointment:

Call the chosen auto repair shop and inquire about their availability. Explain the issue with your car and let them know you'd like to schedule an appointment for repairs. They will likely ask for some details about your vehicle and the type of damage it has sustained.

4.Drop-off and Assessment:

On the day of your appointment, bring your damaged car to the repair shop at the agreed-upon time. A technician will assess the damage and provide an estimate for the repairs. The shop will usually work with your insurance company to finalize the claim and proceed with the repairs.
