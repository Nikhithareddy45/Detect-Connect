Detection of Cars by capturing photo

Detecting the cars by capturing photos is a technologically advanced approach that leverages Artificial Intelligence and computer vision techniques.Our innovative system makes it easy to detect vehicle damage by simply taking a photo of the car from your smartphone. Our cutting-edge AI technology reduces the need for human intervention and streamlines the vehicle repair process.

Process

Photo Capture

Capturing a photo of a car to detect damage involves using a camera or a mobile device to take pictures of the vehicle from various angles.

Detection

Detection on photo capture involves the use of image processing algorithms to identify and analyze potential damages in an image.

GPS Location

GPS location tracking of a car involves using Global Positioning System technology to monitor and record the vehicle's real-time geographical coordinates.
